Thanksgiving Focaccia

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 10 ounces brie
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh cranberries

Method

    Make the focaccia dough
  1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachament, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, tough i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incoporate the last of the flour...
